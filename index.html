Placement · Data & AI Analyst

Leadership Reporting Agent · Stature Leadership

Problem
Coaches spent hours turning leadership survey data into client-ready reports, time that came out of coaching sessions.
Built
A 3+1 agent reporting system: three pillar agents (Awareness, Influence, Resilience) and an orchestrator, grounded in a curated knowledge base and a scoring engine (repeated-measures ANOVA, regression, impact scores). Coaches review and refine every draft, then export to Word and PDF.
Outcome
End-to-end automation from survey intake to emailed reports, cutting manual workload and turnaround time while keeping coaches in control.
